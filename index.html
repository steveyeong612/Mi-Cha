FAQ

Common Questions

What does "ceremonial grade" actually mean? +

Ceremonial grade is the highest quality classification of matcha, used in traditional Japanese tea ceremonies. Made from the youngest shade-grown leaves and stone-ground into an ultra-fine powder, it produces a naturally smooth, umami-rich flavour with minimal bitterness.

What ube extract do you use? +

We use Bionutricia ube purple yam extract powder — a premium, natural extract that delivers authentic ube flavour without artificial colouring or additives. The result is a vibrant, creamy latte with genuine earthy-sweet depth.

How do I place an order? +

Tap the "Order Now" button or any drink's order button — it will open a WhatsApp chat with your drink pre-filled. Choose your milk (oat or almond) and quantity before sending. We'll confirm and arrange delivery or pick-up.

What is the difference between Signature and Origin matcha? +

Both use ceremonial-grade matcha but differ in character. Signature is smooth and balanced — gently umami with aromatic softness, ideal for everyday enjoyment. Origin is bolder with a pronounced green tea character, deeper bitterness and a lingering finish.
